- `sandboxes/worldgen/` — Interactive terrain generation sandbox with Whittaker-style continent/island/highland presets, elevation bands (Water/Lowland/Hills/Mountains), slope, soil fertility/hold, roughness, and precomputed downhill routing fields. Nine overlay modes (Height, Slope, ElevBand, DistOcean, DistWater, SoilFertility, SoilHold, Roughness). Live parameter tweaking for WaterLevel, NoiseScale, ContinentThreshold, RidgeStrength.
- `sandboxes/weather/` — Atmospheric weather system layered on worldgen terrain. Static bake: latitude temperature, 3-cell wind model, orographic precipitation sweeps, storminess, rain shadow. Atmosphere simulation (`atmosphere.h/cpp`): coarse-grid (4× tile scale) atmospheric state with T/q/p/wind/cloud/precip_rate/storminess per cell, latent heat from condensation, solar forcing, temperature relaxation toward T_ref, east-west periodic boundary advection, thermal turbulence with convective gusts; auto-starts after bake. Dynamic simulation: precipitation as rain (capped at 1.0)/snow, snowmelt, infiltration, evaporation, surface water downhill runoff, terrain humidity feedback; reads from atmosphere when active. Telemetry: `AtmosphereStats` (T/q/cloud/precip/wind/pressure/storm ranges, raining_cells, cloudy_cells), live legend panel, console diagnostics every 2 sim-days. Overlay keys 1-9, 0, -, = covering Temperature through SnowDepth; Moisture shows atmospheric humidity (q), RainShadow traces current wind field. Controls: T=play/pause, R=reset dynamics, Numpad+/-=speed.
- Physical invariant enforcement in `sandboxes/weather/` atmosphere simulation: Stefan-Boltzmann T^4 radiative cooling, water mass conservation monitoring with drift correction (>1% threshold), Clausius-Clapeyron q_sat hard clamp, wind speed cap (MAX_WIND_SPEED=6), explicit Laplacian heat and moisture diffusion with periodic wrap, convective instability release, soil-moisture-limited evaporation, CFL/Courant advection limiter, and surface water overflow-to-soil rerouting. `AtmosphereStats` extended with `total_atmo_water`, `max_courant`, `water_correction` fields for live invariant diagnostics.
- `sandboxes/weather/` atmosphere simulation tuning and bug fixes: restructured tick order so solar temperature forcing runs AFTER advection (critical — prevented advection from overwriting forced equatorial temperatures with stale upwind values, collapsing the latitude gradient); added partial temperature advection (15% local retention) to preserve latitude gradient against numerical diffusion; added minimum equatorial Coriolis (0.04 floor) to deflect meridional flow into zonal trade winds; retuned physics constants (K_SOLAR=8.0, K_RELAX_T=0.05 nonlinear, K_DAMP=0.05, ADVECT_SPEED=0.5) for climate stability; tightened water drift correction threshold to 0.1%.
- `sandboxes/weather/` headless diagnostic mode: `sandbox_weather --headless N` runs N sim-days without an SDL2 window and prints per-2-day latitude-band temperature and wind diagnostics, then warns if temperature spread, band spread, or wind speed fall below stability thresholds. Enables atmosphere simulation verification in headless/CI environments.
- `sandboxes/weather/` `AtmosphereStats` extended with `T_band[3]`, `wind_band[3]`, `q_band[3]` arrays (north polar / equatorial / south polar thirds) for latitude-band diagnostics in both headless output and console diagnostic dumps.
- `sandboxes/veggen/` — Vegetation simulation across 8 biome presets (namespace `veggen`). Two-layer moisture model (surface_water + root_moisture), hydrology pipeline (rain→surface→infiltration→root, evaporation reduced by canopy, runoff to downhill neighbors), canopy cover feedback. Five death causes (Drought, Cold, Heat, Competition, Senescence). Overlays: None, RootMoisture, Temperature, Health, Elevation, SurfaceWater, CanopyCover (keys 1-7). Headless modes: `--auto` (seasonal BMP screenshots), `--metrics` (CSV export). Metrics snapshots every 30 sim-days.

- `sandboxes/worldgen/` and `sandboxes/weather/` tectonic plate terrain generation rewrite: `generate_tectonic_terrain()` in both `terrain_gen.cpp` files completely overhauled with a multi-stage physically-based pipeline. (1) Plate generation with deterministic inward velocity bias for continental plates (supercontinent assembly) and outward bias for oceanic plates (seafloor spreading). (2) Domain-warped Voronoi: two-layer warp (WARP1: 35-tile amplitude at 0.005 freq, WARP2: 12-tile at 0.012 freq) applied before Voronoi plate lookup to curve all boundaries; per-tile boundary tangent and convergence field stored for stage 3. (3) Continuous tectonic fields: crustal thickness noise (seed+3000, 0.008 freq) + Gaussian boundary blending for smooth continental-to-oceanic transitions; continental shield uplift (broad Gaussian, σ=80 tiles); anisotropic ridged noise sampled along boundary tangent (5:1 stretch ratio) + cross-noise for irregular mountain ranges; boundary type classification: continent-continent (wide plateau PLATEAU_WIDTH=65 + peaks MOUNTAIN_WIDTH=35, MOUNTAIN_PEAK=0.55), ocean-continent (coastal arc + offshore trench), ocean-ocean (island arc), continental rift (depression + rift shoulders), mid-ocean ridge (central uplift), transform (minor scarp); secondary ridge spurs in foothills zone; micro-detail FBm noise scaled by elevation. (4) Thermal erosion: 5 iterations of steepest-descent material transfer with elevation-dependent rock hardness (THERMAL_RATE=0.20, MAX_STABLE_SLOPE=0.05) + sediment deposition smoothing (15% Laplacian blend per iteration). Constants updated: `CONT_BASE = max(0.58, water_level+0.12)`, `OCEAN_BASE = 0.22`, `MOUNTAIN_WIDTH = 35`, `MOUNTAIN_PEAK = 0.55`, `RIFT_WIDTH = 20`, `PLATEAU_WIDTH = 65`, `TRENCH_DEPTH = 0.06`.
- `sandboxes/worldgen/` headless BMP export: `--headless` CLI flag generates terrain, renders a 1-pixel-per-tile hillshade BMP (NW directional lighting, 315° azimuth / 45° altitude), prints generation stats to stdout, and exits. Output: `terrain_{preset}_{seed}.bmp`.
- `sandboxes/worldgen/` and `sandboxes/weather/` tectonic pipeline improvements (synced to both `terrain_gen.cpp` copies): (a) **Continuous continental field** — plate type is now blended with `cont_warp_noise` (FBm, seed+4000) so continent shapes diverge organically from plate polygon boundaries, producing realistic irregular coastlines independent of Voronoi cells. (b) **Basin depressions** — `basin_noise` (FBm, seed+7000, 0.015 freq) creates inland depressions on continental tiles; strong negative values carve below water level to form lakes; moderate values form shallow valleys. (c) **Large-scale terrain variation** — `terrain_var_noise` (FBm, seed+6000, 0.005 freq) breaks flat continental interiors into rolling hills and plains. (d) **Coastline fractal detail** — `coast_noise` (5-octave FBm, seed+8000, 0.025 freq) adds bays, peninsulas, and small island chains to tiles near the water level boundary. (e) **Hydraulic erosion** — 3-iteration flow accumulation + valley carving pass after thermal erosion; log₂(flow) depth carves V-shaped river valleys and deepens natural basins; high-elevation terrain resists erosion via rock hardness factor; sediment deposits to steepest-descent neighbor (35% of carved material). (f) **Stronger domain warping** — WARP1_STRENGTH increased from 35→55, WARP1_FREQ changed from 0.005→0.004, WARP2_STRENGTH from 12→18 for more organic plate boundary curves. (g) **Foreland and back-arc basins** — convergent boundaries create depression zones: continent-continent collisions add a foreland basin behind the range; ocean-continent subduction adds a back-arc basin on the continental side. (h) **Material-conserving thermal erosion** — sediment now deposited proportionally across all lower neighbors (weighted by excess) instead of being discarded; deposit amounts are tracked separately per iteration. (i) **Detail noise octave increase** — micro-detail FBm increased from 5→6 octaves for finer surface detail. (j) **MOUNTAIN_PEAK updated** from 0.55→0.60. (k) **SDL_Init fix** in `sandboxes/worldgen/main.cpp` — `SDL_Init` now called before headless surface operations (was previously skipped in headless path, causing undefined behaviour). (l) **`--headless` documented** in `config.cpp` `print_usage`.

- `sandboxes/worldgen/` and `sandboxes/weather/` terrain pipeline round 3 — geomorphology improvements (synced to both `terrain_gen.cpp`): (a) **Distance-from-coast inland bias** (step 6) — BFS from coastal land tiles + exponential saturation (`0.12 * (1 - exp(-dist/15))`) pushes elevation inland, preventing mountains from hugging coastlines. (b) **Multi-scale coastal roughness** (step 7) — two FBm noise layers (medium 0.035 freq + broad 0.015 freq) applied within ±0.12 height of water level create bays, peninsulas, and archipelago-scale coastal features. (c) **Basin flood-fill lakes** (step 8) — priority-queue basin detection from local minima, flood-fills up to spill points with generation-counter visited tracking, caps at 2000 tiles per basin, enforces minimum lake size of 4 tiles; ocean-connected basins correctly skipped. (d) **Single-tile water speckle removal** (step 9) — connected-component analysis removes water bodies smaller than 3 tiles. (e) **Hydraulic erosion strengthened** — 6 iterations at 0.008 strength (was 3 at 0.005) for visible drainage valleys. (f) **Relief-based band thresholds tightened** — mountains require relief > 0.22 AND slope > 0.18 (was 0.18/0.15), hills require relief > 0.12 (was 0.10).

### Changed
- `sandboxes/worldgen/` and `sandboxes/weather/` geology-first terrain refactor: hydrology (rivers, lakes, D8 downhill routing, flow accumulation) removed from terrain generation entirely; rivers and lakes now emerge dynamically through WSE-based multi-neighbor flow and groundwater baseflow in the weather dynamics simulation. `TerrainTile` loses `is_lake`, `dist_water`, `soil_fertility`, `soil_hold`, `downhill_x/y`, `river_flow`; gains `RockType rock`, `SoilTexture soil`, `bedrock_hardness`, `bedrock_permeability`, `soil_depth`, `porosity`, `ksat`, `field_capacity`, `wilting_point`, `erodibility`. Worldgen overlays change: removed DistWater, SoilFertility, SoilHold, RiverFlow; added Geology (categorical RockType) and SoilTexture (categorical). Total overlays now 9 (keys 1-9). `GenerationTimings`: `ocean_lake_ms` renamed to `ocean_ms`; `soil_ms`, `downhill_ms`, `river_ms` removed; `geology_ms` added. `AtmosphereCell` loses `avg_river_flow`. Weather dynamics gains `groundwater` reservoir + baseflow discharge per tile; infiltration uses geology-based `ksat` + `field_capacity`; `DynamicTile` adds `groundwater` and `discharge`; `DynamicStats` adds `groundwater_mean/max` and `discharge_mean/max`.
- `sandboxes/worldgen/` renamed generation types: `world_gen.h/cpp` → `terrain_gen.h/cpp`, `World` struct → `Terrain`, `generate_world` → `generate_terrain`, `WorldStats` → `TerrainStats`. Weather sandbox synced to the same rename (`world_gen.h/cpp` → `terrain_gen.h/cpp`). These renames bring naming in line with what the sandbox actually generates (terrain, not a full game world).
- `sandboxes/worldgen/` overlay legend panels now show a short info description line beneath the gradient bar or swatches, explaining what the field represents and how it was computed (applies to all 11 overlays).
- `sandboxes/worldgen/` camera now starts fitted to show the full world at launch via `Camera::fit_world` — no need to zoom out manually.
- `sandboxes/worldgen/` terrain pipeline overhauled: domain warping added to height field generation (two Perlin displacement layers warp input coordinates up to 20 tiles for organic landforms); ridge field merged into unified height so mountains affect hydrology, slope, distance fields, and band classification; Euclidean Distance Transform (Felzenszwalb separable parabola-envelope) replaces BFS Manhattan distances for `dist_ocean` and `dist_water`; aspect field added (`atan2` slope direction, [-π, π]); roughness changed from ridged-noise+slope blend to 3×3 neighborhood height standard deviation; downhill routing upgraded to 8-directional D8 with lake outflow spill points; flow accumulation implemented replacing the previous stub (river_flow was 0 in all tiles). Two new overlay modes added: Aspect (key 0) and RiverFlow (key -). `TerrainTile` gained `aspect`, `downhill_x`, `downhill_y` fields. `GenerationTimings` now has `ridge_ms`, `unify_ms`, `downhill_ms`, `river_ms` stages (replacing old `elevation_ms`).
- `sandboxes/weather/` terrain generation synced with worldgen pipeline changes (domain warping, unified height, aspect, D8 downhill, flow accumulation, EDT distance fields).
- `sandboxes/weather/` temperature range widened: `T_ref` formula changed from `-10 + solar * 35` to `-15 + solar_seasonal * 45` with stronger seasonal latitude modulation (`* 12` vs `* 8`). This enables realistic heat waves (equatorial T > 30°C in summer) and deeper polar cold, giving the atmosphere a wider and more realistic temperature range.
- `sandboxes/weather/` storminess formula rewritten from additive (which saturated ~90% of cells, making storminess uniform and meaningless) to multiplicative with convergence threshold: `humidity_ratio * instability^3 * (1 + cloud*0.5)` where instability requires wind convergence > 0.2. Produces realistic ~15-19% severe storm cell coverage concentrated in meteorologically active zones.
- `sandboxes/weather/` headless diagnostic final summary now reports cumulative peak extremes tracked across the entire simulation run — peak T range, peak heat wave / deep freeze / severe storm / gale / heavy rain cell counts, and peak per-cell precipitation intensity — rather than a snapshot of the final timestep only.
- `sandboxes/weather/` precipitation is now intermittent: smooth bilinear noise (10-cell + 4-cell scales, drifting with wind, evolving every ~12 sim-hours) gates rain delivery — only ~45% of cloudy cells rain at any given time, creating coherent weather fronts rather than uniform drizzle across all cloudy cells. Rain intensity is 2.5× higher when triggered to preserve total moisture budget. Orographic precipitation (mountain uplift) remains continuous. Headless per-2-day diagnostics now report cloudy cell count alongside raining cell count to monitor spatial patchiness.
- `sandboxes/weather/` precipitation model replaced: noise-gated intermittency removed in favour of a physics-based cloud lifecycle. Noisy condensation thresholds cause cloud to thicken; precipitation falls when cloud exceeds CLOUD_PRECIP_THRESHOLD (0.12) at CLOUD_PRECIP_RATE (1.2), then cloud dissipates via CLOUD_EVAP_BASE (0.15), CLOUD_EVAP_DRY (0.40), and pressure-driven CLOUD_SUBSIDE_RATE (0.50). Condensation noise uses cubic Hermite temporal crossfade between timesteps to prevent global pattern teleport; a second decorrelated noise layer (spatial offset +1000) prevents it from reinforcing the first. `hash01()` changed to `uint32_t` parameters to fix signed integer overflow UB. OCEAN_EVAP_RATE reduced from 0.35 → 0.15; LAND_EVAP_RATE = 0.05 added. T_ref solar multiplier increased from 45 → 48.
- `sandboxes/weather/` Precipitation and Storminess overlays now use `AtmosphereState::sample_nearest()` (new method) instead of bilinear `sample()`, producing sharp shower boundaries that match the underlying coarse-grid cell structure rather than smeared gradients.
- `sandboxes/weather/` atmosphere model upgraded with five-phase improvement plan: (Phase 0) terrain coupling in tick step 1 — wind-slope orographic uplift, aspect-modulated solar heating, maritime moderation by distance-to-ocean, river-evaporation feedback, pressure diffusion, storm persistence; (Phase 1) moisture budget field `precip_budget` [0..2] advected with wind, depleted by rain, recharged over ocean; (Phase 2) climate baseline / weather anomaly split — `T_base`/`q_base`/`p_base` hold slow seasonal state, `T_anom`/`q_anom`/`p_anom` hold fast advected anomalies, composite T/q/p updated end-of-tick; (Phase 3) two-layer atmosphere — upper free troposphere fields (`T_upper`, `q_upper`, `u_upper`, `v_upper`, `p_upper`, `stability`) with vertical exchange between layers; (Phase 4) energy conservation tracking via `AtmosphereState::initial_energy` / `total_energy`. `AtmosphereStats` extended with budget stats, anomaly stddev, upper layer stats (T_upper_mean, wind_upper_mean/max, stability_min/max/mean, inversion_cells), and energy budget (solar_in, radiative_out, latent_release, energy_drift_pct). New overlays: PrecipBudget (backtick), UpperWind (F1), Stability (F2). New renderer color functions: `precip_budget_color`, `stability_color`.
- `docs/systems/` overhauled: removed five stale/unimplemented system docs (PLANETARY_MODEL.md, NUTRIENT_REGENERATION.md, NEURAL_EVOLUTION.md, WORLD_GENERATION.md, SPRITE_GENERATION.md); replaced with three docs covering sandbox-prototyped systems (TERRAIN_GENERATION.md, WEATHER_SYSTEM.md, PLANT_GENERATION.md).

- `sandboxes/worldgen/` and `sandboxes/weather/` tectonic pipeline improvements (synced to both `terrain_gen.cpp` copies): (a) **Continental spines** — each continental plate computes a spine direction perpendicular to its velocity (`spine_dx`, `spine_dy`, `spine_offset`); a Gaussian ridge (σ=25 tiles) runs through the continental interior, uplift up to `0.15 × ridge_strength` modulated by `ridge_noise` for peaks/gaps and attenuated toward plate margins. Simulates Andes/Rockies-style interior ranges. (b) **Meso-scale ridge noise** — `meso_ridge_noise` (ridged fractal, 3 octaves, 0.012 freq, seed+8000) replaces the removed `coast_noise` at the same seed slot; adds interior escarpments and secondary ridge arcs within continental interiors (up to 0.08 per tile). (c) **Removed coast_noise** — the 5-octave FBm coastline noise (0.025 freq) that caused "fried edge" pixel speckle on coastlines has been removed. Coastlines now emerge organically from the continuous continental field and domain warping. (d) **Stronger boundary foothill envelopes** — continent-continent collisions gain a Gaussian foothill envelope (σ=90 tiles, amplitude `0.06 × strength`); ocean-continent subduction gains a foothill envelope (σ=80 tiles, amplitude `0.05 × strength`); subduction mountain peak multiplier increased to 1.1× (was 1.0×). (e) **Terrain variation strengthened** — `terrain_var_noise` frequency lowered 0.005→0.004, land scale increased 0.10→0.12; `basin_noise` frequency lowered 0.015→0.012, max depression strength increased 0.12→0.14. (f) **Relief-based elevation band classification** — `compute_slope_aspect_bands()` now computes `local_relief` (max−min in R=4 neighborhood) and classifies: Mountains = relief > 0.22 AND slope > 0.18; Hills = relief > 0.12 OR (slope > 0.12 AND elevation > water_level+0.12); Lowland = everything else above water. Replaces the old absolute-elevation thresholds (water_level+0.18 for hills, +0.30 for mountains).

Static bake moisture formula corrected from `precip - evap * 0.5` (arbitrary) to `precip / (evap + 0.01) * 0.8` (proper aridity index ratio), seeding initial tile aridity correctly.

- `sandboxes/worldgen/` and `sandboxes/weather/` terrain pipeline round 4 — geomorphic detail improvements (synced to both `terrain_gen.cpp` copies): (a) **Pipeline expanded to 12 stages** — two new stages inserted between hydraulic erosion and the coast bias pass. (b) **Valley-floor smoothing (stage 5b)** — targeted low-pass filter applied after hydraulic erosion softens V-valley floors into broader U-profiles and smooths alluvial deposits without affecting ridges or peaks. (c) **Noise-warped coastal plain enforcement (stage 6b)** — tiles within a noise-warped nearshore band (1.5–7 tile width per tile) are lowered to form flat coastal lowlands; FBm warp on the band boundary creates irregular coastline transitions; steep coasts are skipped to preserve cliffs and fjords. (d) **Coastal morphological smoothing (stage 10)** — final morphological pass blends elevation transitions at the coastal plain edge to remove staircasing artifacts. (e) **Province/craton mask** — per-tile tectonic activity score (derived from convergence_field and boundary proximity) gates `meso_ridge_noise`, `terrain_var_noise`, and 6-octave detail noise; active zones receive full amplitude, stable cratons receive reduced variation — prevents texture noise from washing out continent-scale structure. (f) **Along-strike mountain belt segmentation** — `belt_var` noise now modulates peak height with ±40% amplitude along the range strike direction at continent-continent boundaries, breaking ranges into distinct massifs and lower saddles rather than uniform ridges. (g) **Wider continental spines with fragmentation** — spine Gaussian envelope widened from σ=25 → σ=40 tiles; along-strike fragmentation noise (samples ridge_noise along the spine axis) creates peaks and saddles, producing more realistic interior range structure. (h) **River rendering in headless BMP** — tiles with `river_flow ≥ 100` cells drawn in bright blue with sqrt-based intensity; major arteries visually distinct from tributaries. (i) **Continental shelf in headless BMP** — shallow ocean tiles near coast tinted lighter blue to indicate shelf zone. (j) **Hillshade amplification reduced** from 8× → 5× in headless BMP rendering to recover detail in low-relief terrain.

- `sandboxes/worldgen/` coastline geomorphology improvements — coastal zone of `terrain_gen.cpp` overhauled with process-based coastal shaping and new pipeline stages: **(5d) Valley drowning** — rias, estuaries, and fjords created by selectively lowering valley floors near the coast below water level using flow accumulation, regional sea-level anomaly noise, and fjord detection (high relief + high latitude). Produces dendritic drowned-valley inlets. **(7, replaced) Exposure-based SDF coastal remodeling** — previous uniform coastal fractalization removed; new four-step process: (7a) BFS signed distance field from coastline; (7b) wave exposure via 16-direction fetch raymarching (max 60 tiles); (7c) coastal slope proxy + 3-pass Gaussian exposure blur; (7d) exposure-driven anisotropic displacement with spectral control — sheltered coasts suppress high-freq 90%, mid 70%, broad 30%. (7e) Gated coastal profile ramp (1–3 tile width, 50% blend, respects cliffs). **(7b, new) Depositional coastal features** — river-mouth deltas (fan-shaped lobes with distributary channels) on high-flow coasts; barrier islands (offshore ridges on moderate-exposure coasts with tidal inlet gaps). **(7c, new) Clustered skerry generation** on exposed headlands — Poisson-like cluster centers with 8-tile minimum spacing, each spawning islet fields within a 3–6 tile radius. **(7d, new) Sub-bay inlet carving** in sheltered concavities — traces upstream along steepest-ascent for up to 8 tiles to create fjord-like or estuary-like recesses in low-exposure bays. **(9b, new) Debris islet removal** — land bodies smaller than 8 tiles (smaller than existing speckle threshold) removed as visual noise. **(9c, new) Morphological narrow feature cleanup** — neck removal (1-tile-wide land necks between water bodies), corner bridge removal (diagonal connectivity only), and peninsula tip pruning to clean up degenerate coastal geometry. **(10, replaced) SDF curvature flow smoothing** — Laplacian smoothing replaced with 4-iteration SDF curvature flow (dt=0.18) restricted to a 3-tile coastal band, producing anti-aliased coastlines with organic curvature. Minor rendering tweaks in `main.cpp` for the new coastal feature types.

- `sandboxes/worldgen/` terrain pipeline round 5 — geological detail and headless rendering improvements: (a) **Stronger Voronoi domain warping** — WARP1_STRENGTH increased 55→65, WARP2_STRENGTH 18→25 for more organic plate boundary geometry. (b) **Boundary distance noise** — moderate FBm noise added to raw `boundary_dist` to produce wavy, irregular Voronoi edges rather than linear transitions. (c) **Boundary normal rotation** — per-tile rotation noise (up to ~35° total from two noise layers) applied to the boundary normal/tangent vectors, breaking straight-line plate boundary alignment without destroying structural coherence. (d) **Curl noise warping of ridge coordinates** — two OpenSimplex2 FBm layers (seed+26000/26001, 22-tile displacement) warp the coordinate space used to sample ridge noise, breaking linear ridge ridgelines into organic curved ranges. (e) **Lithology noise** — OpenSimplex2 FBm (seed+27000, 0.005 freq) spatially varies the effective ridge frequency per region, simulating different rock types and producing heterogeneous ridge spacing. (f) **Piedmont foothill roughness** — OpenSimplex2 FBm (seed+28000, 0.025 freq) applied at mountain margins in the foothill zone, adding coarse roughness characteristic of real piedmont deposits. (g) **Multi-direction ridge blending** — boundary ridge noise now blends three components: 40% primary direction + 25% rotated 30° + 35% isotropic, preventing ridges from always aligning perfectly parallel to plate boundaries. (h) **Three-scale relief domain warping (step 3b)** — after stage 3, a three-scale domain warp (12 + 5 + 2 tile displacement) applied to the height field to break any remaining linear tectonic artifacts. (i) **Stream-power erosion with sediment capacity** — new 40-iteration stream-power law erosion replaces simple 6-iteration hydraulic erosion; each iteration: D8 flow accumulation, stream-power carving proportional to flow × slope, sediment transport and deposition with explicit capacity model (sediment deposited when stream loses power). Rock hardness and mountain canyon boost (2× carving for high-elevation high-flow tiles) applied per tile. (j) **Reduced thermal erosion** — iterations reduced from 5→6 to 6 with gentler parameters (rate 0.12, threshold 0.055) to complement the new stream-power erosion without over-smoothing. (k) **Stronger valley carving with canyon boost** — stream-power carving coefficient raised; high-elevation high-flow tiles receive 2× carving boost to produce dramatic mountain canyons. (l) **Tectonics-aware bathymetry** — ocean floor coloring in headless BMP now uses convergence zone and continental margin information: river shelf boost (river-adjacent shallow ocean extends shelf), margin-dependent shelf width (convergent margins get narrower shelves, passive margins wider), and convergent margin trenches added as darker bands. (m) **Cubic coast suppression with wider range** — revised coast suppression formula uses a cubic falloff over a wider depth range to eliminate the abrupt coastal "ring" artifact. (n) **Valley-floor smoothing preserves mountain detail** — smoothing strength now scaled by elevation so high-elevation terrain receives near-zero smoothing, preserving mountain structure while only flattening low-elevation valley floors.
- `sandboxes/worldgen/` headless BMP rendering improvements: (a) **Gamma-compressed ocean depth coloring** — depth mapped through `d^0.6` gamma before applying quadratic color gradient, so the shelf-to-abyss transition is smooth without a bright coastal halo. (b) **6× hillshade slope exaggeration** — slope_angle uses `slope_mag * 6.0` (was 5×) for slightly more pronounced hill shading. (c) **Climate-driven biome coloring** — land pixels now colored by a weighted blend of seven biome types (tundra, boreal, alpine, arid, steppe, tropical, temperate), each computed from a climate model using latitude + lapse rate for temperature and a composite moisture model (dist_ocean + latitude + slope + continental drying + soil proxy + roughness noise). Logistic functions with visible ecotone widths produce smooth biome transitions. (d) **Aspect-based snow accumulation** — snow coverage now depends on slope steepness (steep faces shed snow), elevation ramp, pole-facing aspect preference, and moisture; blue-white tint varies with temperature.
